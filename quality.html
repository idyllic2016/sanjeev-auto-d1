Quality Philosophy

Built-In Quality.
Not Inspected-In.

Our quality management system is built on prevention, not detection. Every process is designed with quality built-in — from tool selection to cutting parameters to handling procedures.

01

Advanced Product Quality Planning (APQP)

Structured quality planning from design stage through production launch. Risk identification and mitigation before first article approval.

02

Statistical Process Control (SPC)

Real-time monitoring of critical dimensions using automated gauging systems. Cpk targets of 1.67 minimum for critical characteristics.

03

First Article Inspection (FAI)

100% dimensional verification of first articles per PPAP Level 3. Full material certification and process capability studies.

04

CMM & 3D Metrology

Zeiss and Hexagon CMMs for sub-micron dimensional verification. Gear measurement on Klingelnberg and Gleason testers.

05

Non-Destructive Testing

Magnetic particle inspection, dye penetrant testing, and ultrasonic examination for critical components.

06

Customer-Specific Requirements

Full compliance with CSR for TATA, Mahindra, AGCO, CNH, and other major OEM customers. Customised control plans and inspection methods.
