Quality Philosophy

Quality Built In.
Not Inspected In.

Our INPUT-PROCESS-OUTPUT quality system is built on prevention, not detection. Every incoming material is spectrographically analysed before it enters our production floor. Every process parameter is monitored in real-time using SPC charting and robotic gauging.

Our OUTPUT verification uses Klingelnberg gear analysers, Zeiss Contura CMMs, and TTI gear testers — supported by monthly zero-defect reviews and continuous Six Sigma improvement cycles under JIPM TPM Excellence.

High-precision CNC gear measuring machine for complete gear geometry analysis including profile, lead, pitch, and runout measurements to DIN/ISO standards.

Bridge-type coordinate measuring machine for sub-micron dimensional verification. Full GD&T analysis capability including form, position, and orientation tolerances.
