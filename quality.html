Quality Process

End-to-End Quality
Control Process

01

Incoming Material QC

100% incoming raw material inspection against material test certificates. Spectrometer analysis for chemical composition. Segregated storage with lot traceability.

02

In-Process Inspection

SPC at critical operations. First-off and last-off inspection protocols. Poka-yoke mistake-proofing at every stage. Real-time quality data capture on production lines.

03

Final Inspection

Zeiss CMM 3D inspection. Klingelnberg gear analyser for gear tooth geometry. Surface roughness, hardness, and dimensional verification. Customer-specific inspection plans.

04

PPAP & Documentation

Full PPAP Level 3 documentation available. Control plans, MSA, SPC studies, PFMEA, and DFMEA. Digital traceability from raw material to shipped component.

Quality Policy

Our Commitment
to Excellence

Sanjeev Auto is committed to achieving and sustaining Total Customer Satisfaction by producing precision components that consistently meet or exceed specified quality requirements.

We achieve this through a culture of continuous improvement, employee engagement, adoption of advanced manufacturing technology, and adherence to international quality management systems across all our facilities.

Our quality policy is reviewed annually by top management and cascaded through all levels of the organization — from the shop floor to the boardroom.

Six Sigma Driven

Data-Driven Quality Improvement

Active Six Sigma Green Belt and Black Belt programs. Continuous reduction of defect rates using DMAIC methodology.

TPM Excellence

Recognised by Japan Institute of Plant Maintenance for Total Productive Maintenance excellence. OEE targets consistently above 85%.

Zero PPM Goal

Customer Zero Defect Commitment

Customer-specific PPM targets with real-time dashboards. 8D corrective action process for any customer concern — resolved within defined response timelines.
