Our Story

A legacy built on
uncompromising
precision

From a single workshop in 1978 to a 5-plant manufacturing group, Sanjeev Auto has grown by doing one thing exceptionally well — crafting precision gears and transmission components that global OEMs can bet their equipment on.

Every component we deliver carries 45 years of metallurgical expertise, rigorous process discipline, and an obsession with zero-defect output that has made us the partner of choice for John Deere, Mahindra, CLAAS, ZF, and 17 more leading manufacturers.

Manufacturing Excellence

End-to-End
Manufacturing Mastery

From raw steel to finished precision component — our integrated capabilities eliminate supply chain risk and ensure consistent quality at every stage.

Gear Cutting & Hobbing

High-precision spur, helical, and bevel gear cutting with automated CNC hobbing centers. Modules 1–8, DIN Q7 accuracy standard.

CNC Automated

Profile & Cylindrical Grinding

Sub-micron surface finishing with CNC profile grinders and cylindrical grinding centers. Tolerances down to ±0.002mm.

±0.002mm Tolerance

Gear Honing & Shaving

Post-heat-treatment precision honing for flawless tooth surface finish, noise reduction, and extended gear life.

Post-Heat Treatment

Spline Rolling & Forming

Cold-roll forming of external and internal splines, providing superior strength-to-weight characteristics vs. cut splines.

Cold Roll Forming

CMM & Metrology

Zeiss CMM machines, TTI gear testers, Klingenberg analyzers, and profile projectors for comprehensive dimensional verification.

Zeiss CMM

Metallurgical Lab

In-house spectro analysis, hardness testing, micro-structure analysis, and material certification for complete traceability.

Full Traceability
