Spur & Helical Gears

Precision-hobbed and ground gears manufactured on Liebherr gear hobbing machines. ISO Grade 5–7 accuracy. Module range: 1.5 – 12. Suitable for automotive transmissions and agricultural drives.

Bevel & Worm Gears

High-precision bevel and worm gear sets for differential and axle applications. Spiral bevel gears manufactured on Gleason machines to AGMA/DIN standards. Heat treated for superior fatigue life.

DifferentialAxleAGMA/DIN

Precision Shafts

Splined, stepped, and hollow transmission shafts. Roll-forged splines for superior fatigue strength. CNC turned and ground to Ra 0.8 μm or better. Custom specifications available.

SplinedSteppedHollow

Precision-machined gearbox shifter forks for manual and automated manual transmissions. Die-forged steel blanks, precision machined to critical engagement dimensions. Supplied to major OEM transmission lines.
