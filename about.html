Our Story

Built On Precision.

Four decades of engineering excellence from the precision manufacturing hub of Aurangabad, Maharashtra.

The Foundation

Where It All
Began — 1978.

Founded in 1978 in Aurangabad, Maharashtra, Sanjeev Auto Industries has grown from a single-facility gear manufacturer into a 5-plant precision engineering powerhouse — trusted by the world's most demanding OEMs across automotive, agricultural, and industrial sectors.

Headquartered at C-7, MIDC Waluj, Aurangabad — India's precision manufacturing corridor — we supply 1,000+ component variants to John Deere, Mahindra, Kubota, ZF, GM, Eaton and 20+ global partners.

Growth Milestones

Four Decades of
Engineering Progress

1978

Company Founded

Sanjeev Auto Industries established in Aurangabad, Maharashtra. Began as a precision gear manufacturer for local OEMs.

1990s

First OEM Partnerships

Began supplying to Mahindra and Bajaj — establishing Sanjeev Auto as a quality Tier-1 component supplier.

2000s

Capacity Expansion

Multiple MIDC Waluj facilities commissioned. 200+ CNC machines. Heat treatment and gear grinding centres established.

2010

Global OEM Onboarding

John Deere, Kubota, ZF Group and General Motors added as customers. Export volumes scale significantly.

2015

IATF 16949 Certified

Achieved the automotive industry's highest quality management standard, unlocking new global supply chain access.

2018

JIPM TPM Excellence Award

Recognised by Japan Institute of Plant Maintenance for Total Productive Maintenance excellence across all facilities.

2022

G168 Plant Commissioned

Forging and machined castings division expanded. 1600T forging press and 5-axis CNC centres added.

2026

Precision Leadership

5 plants. 1,000+ component variants. 20+ global OEM partners. IATF, ISO 14001, ISO 45001, VDA 6.3 certified.

Corporate Pledge

The P.I.T.C.H. Philosophy

Five values embedded in every process, every component, every relationship at Sanjeev Auto.

Our Vision

To be a global leader in providing end-to-end solutions in Motion Control Applications.

Our Mission

To be a preferred business partner for customers by leveraging a highly engaged team through technology and innovation.

P

Passion

High-level commitment to customer needs. Every component is engineered with the same dedication regardless of volume or complexity.

I

Innovation

Continuous investment in cutting-edge manufacturing technology — Liebherr hobbing, Klingelnberg gear analysers, robotic assembly lines.

T

Transparency

Open, honest communication with customers, suppliers, and employees. Traceability from raw material to final component.

C

Care

Care for Self, Team, Environment, and Society — embedded in every process and every decision across our operations.

H

Happiness

Joy and Satisfaction of all Stakeholders — customers, employees, suppliers, and the communities we operate in.
