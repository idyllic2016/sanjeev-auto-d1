The Foundation

Where It All
Began.

Founded in 1993 with a single CNC machine and a belief that Indian manufacturing could match global precision standards, Sanjeev Auto has grown into a multi-facility enterprise supplying critical components to some of the world's largest automotive and industrial OEMs.

What started as a local gear supplier has become a trusted engineering partner across 25+ countries — driven by relentless investment in technology, talent, and quality systems.

Growth Milestones

Three Decades
Of Engineering Progress

1993

Company Founded

Sanjeev Auto Industries established in Faridabad with a focus on precision gear manufacturing for local OEMs.

1998

ISO 9001 Certification

Achieved first quality certification, opening doors to Tier-1 automotive supply chains.

2004

Plant 2 Commissioned

Expanded to Manesar with dedicated heat treatment and grinding facility, tripling production capacity.

2009

First Export Order

Began exporting precision components to European agricultural machinery manufacturers.

2014

IATF 16949 Certified

Achieved the automotive industry's highest quality standard, enabling global OEM partnerships.

2018

Pune Facility Launch

Plant 3 inaugurated in Pune, serving western India automotive cluster including Tata and Mahindra.

2022

R&D Innovation Hub

Gurugram R&D center opened — focused on EV-compatible components and smart manufacturing.

2026

Global Leader Status

10M+ components annually. 25+ export countries. 2,500+ workforce. ₹500Cr+ revenue.
