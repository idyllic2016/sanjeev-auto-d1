The Foundation

Where It All
Began — 1978.

Founded in 1978 in Aurangabad, Maharashtra, Sanjeev Auto Industries has grown from a single-facility gear manufacturer into a 5-plant precision engineering powerhouse — trusted by the world's most demanding OEMs.

Headquartered at C-7, MIDC Waluj — India's precision manufacturing corridor — we supply 1,000+ component variants to John Deere, Mahindra, Kubota, ZF, GM, Eaton and 20+ global partners.
